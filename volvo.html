The Volvo EX90 is a mid-size luxury electric SUV, based on the XC90 model. It features Volvo's signature Thor's Hammer LED headlights, a blanked-off grille, flush door handles, and 22-inch alloy wheels. The rear has C-shaped LED taillights. Sustainability is a key focus, with 15% recycled steel, 25% recycled aluminum, and 48kg of recycled plastics and bio-based materials. Inside, the seven-seater boasts a tech-centric cabin, highlighted by a 14.5-inch Google OS-powered touchscreen infotainment system with 5G connectivity and over-the-air updates.
