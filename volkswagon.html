Volkswagen ID.4

Price: Rs.: 50.00 - 60.00 Lakh

The Volkswagen ID.4 is set to launch in India by the end of the year, likely in the top-spec GTX variant. The GTX version will feature a dual-tone exterior, 20-inch alloy wheels, pop-out door handles, an all-black interior, a floating touchscreen infotainment system, a three-spoke steering wheel, and a digital instrument cluster. It will be powered by a 77kWh battery pack with two electric motors, generating 299 bhp and 310 Nm of torque, providing a range of 479 km on a full charge, and achieving 0-100 km/h in over six seconds. The ID.4 has earned a five-star rating in the Euro NCAP crash test and will compete with the Hyundai Ioniq 5, Kia EV6, and the upcoming Skoda Enyaq iV.
